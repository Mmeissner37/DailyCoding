Breaking News: Santa has been spotted!

Santa and his team of reindeer were seen flying over a small town in Virginia

By Michaela Meissner

In a shocking display of holiday spirit, Santa was seen flying over smalltown Winchester, VA on Christmas Eve. There has a been a recent, and oftentimes heated, debate about whether or not the jolly holidy maker actually exists. Those arguments are now forever laid to rest after hundreds of witnesses spotted the white-haired man, in his festive red attire, sitting in a sleigh pulled by eight beautiful reindeer.
Santa waved to the rapidly gathering audience as he hovered over the downtown tree lighting ceremony. Some onlookers even swore he winked at them with a sparkle in his eye. More than a dozen women swooned. After seeming to pose for pictures, he gave his reindeer a click of his tongue and off he went. As he disappeared from sight, his jolly voice was heard saying, "Merry Christmas to all! And to all, a good night!"
